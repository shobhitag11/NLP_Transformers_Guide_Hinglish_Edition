## Why I Built This

I saw too many people struggling to learn AI.

Some were watching random YouTube videos.  
Some were jumping between courses.  
Some were reading research papers they barely understood.  

And despite all that… many still couldn’t truly understand what was happening inside modern AI systems.

The problem wasn’t intelligence.

The problem was fragmented AI education.

Too many buzzwords.  
Too many platforms.  
Too much information.  
Too little clarity.

So I decided to build something I genuinely wished existed when I started learning deeply about AI.

A practical, intuition-first Hinglish guide that explains modern AI systems in a way Indian learners naturally connect with.

Not forced Hindi.  
Not robotic English.  

Just natural Hinglish explanations with real intuition, diagrams, mental models, and practical understanding.

# Why Hinglish?

Most Indians naturally think in a mix of Hindi + English while learning technical concepts.

Pure English explanations sometimes feel disconnected.  
Pure Hindi explanations sometimes struggle with technical depth.

Hinglish creates the perfect balance:
- Technical clarity from English
- Natural understanding from Hindi
- Faster mental connection
- Better long-term retention
